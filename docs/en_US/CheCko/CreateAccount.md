# Create account

After installation, CheCko will be run in a Chrome extension environment. But if you don't have any account created or you just installed it, it'll open a new tab in a Chrome as a tutorial for account creation.

## Create password

Your password must contain letters, digits, and symbols. You must store your password properly. CheCko is not able to recover your password if your forget it due to the it only store cipher text for the wallet.

## Backup and validate mnemonic

Currently, the mnemonic can only recover the first generated account. If you create a new account in the wallet again, you cannot recover it with this mnemonic currently. You must store your mnemonic properly outside your CheCko wallet (maybe write it down on a piece of paper). CheCko is not able to recover your account if you lose your mnemonic.

## Backup private key

Besides the mnemonic, you can also backup private keys for all of your accounts in CheCko. Be careful not to leak your private keys to anyone else! People who have this private key can access any assets in this account.
